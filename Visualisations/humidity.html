Humidity vs. Latitude

There appears to be no observed relationship between humidity and latitude within this dataset, with each latitude band showing a range of different humidities.

Humidity can be affected by air temperature, since air temperature determines how much water vapour air can hold. At higher temperatures, air can hold more water vapour, whereas at lower temperatures, air has a much lower humidity capcity. (Source)
